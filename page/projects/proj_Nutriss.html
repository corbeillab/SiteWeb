Nutrition, santé et société (NUTRISS)

The NUTRISS Centre's mission is one of research, of training and of mobilization and transfer of knowledge. The Centre's researchers, by their work, aspire to be a driver of change through their actions based on the needs of individuals, of the environment and of decision makers.
NUTRISS federates and mobilizes the forces of more than 45 senior scientists, of which many are international leaders in their area of expertise. These researchers come from 12 different faculties of Université Laval, which reflects the transdisciplinary nature of the work they conduct in research.
